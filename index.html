Welcome to MUT Ajira Club

A club sponsored by Ajira Digital

Ajira Digital, a program initiated by Kenya's Ministry of Information, Communication, and Digital Economy (MICDE), aims to boost youth employment through online job opportunities. It provides essential training on earning money online from the comfort of one's home. For more information visit Ajira Digital Website.
